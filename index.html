FAQ
Common Questions

How do I book a tattoo session?

Click the "Book a Session" button anywhere on this site. You'll be directed to my booking form where you can describe your idea, share references, and choose your preferred dates. I'll get back to you within 48 hours.

How much does a tattoo cost?

Every piece is unique, so pricing depends on size, detail, placement and number of sessions. After reviewing your idea, I'll provide a personalised quote. A non-refundable deposit is required to secure your booking.

How should I prepare for my session?

Get a good night's sleep, eat a proper meal beforehand, and stay hydrated. Avoid alcohol and blood thinners for 24 hours. Wear comfortable clothing that gives easy access to the tattoo area. Bring snacks and entertainment for longer sessions.

What's the aftercare process?

I'll provide detailed aftercare instructions after your session. Generally: keep the wrap on for the recommended time, wash gently with fragrance-free soap, apply a thin layer of aftercare balm, and avoid sun exposure, swimming and tight clothing for 2-3 weeks.

Do you do cover-ups?

Yes! Cover-ups are one of my favourite challenges. Send me a clear photo of the existing tattoo along with your new idea, and I'll let you know what's possible. Some cover-ups may require a consultation in person.

Are touch-ups included?

One free touch-up is included within 3 months of your original session, provided you've followed the aftercare instructions. Beyond that, touch-ups are charged at a reduced rate.
